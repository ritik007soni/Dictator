# Dictator
It allows users to search for the meaning of a word by simply selecting the word from any text on their device. With this app, users can easily expand their vocabulary and enhance their understanding of various words encountered in their day-to-day reading or conversations.
* Once a word is selected, the app immediately fetches the meaning and relevant information and displays it in a popup window.
* It offers a convenient and efficient way to explore the world of words right at the user's fingertips.
